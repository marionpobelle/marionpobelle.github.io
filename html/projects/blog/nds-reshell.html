NDS Lite Reshell

OVERVIEW

A long time ago, my Nintendo DS Lite's upper screen turned white and I stopped using it after that. I recently got a Nintendo 3DS that could run my old DS Lite games and decided, out of nostalgia, to fix it anyway. So I looked online for what the problem could be and I had 3 options: one the thin cable linking the board to the top screen could be slightly out of its connecting slot. Two, that same cable could simply be broken. Three, rotating a simple brightness adjustment button could do the trick. So I ordered online a bottom touch screen to replace my current overused one, a pink shell to change my old, dirty blue one and to hope for the best for the upper screen issue (the cable was in fact, broken).

So I spent a whole day watching tutorials on how to reshell a NDS Lite and got to work with mine. I couldn't fix my upper screen, but I loved opening the console and tinkering with it!
